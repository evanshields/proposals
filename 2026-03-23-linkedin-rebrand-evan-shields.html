About Section

I started as a teacher on the south side of Atlanta. Every year I taught, rent hikes pushed me out of my housing. I was living the same struggle as the families I served.

That's when I realized: the built environment changes everything. But I couldn't fix it from inside a classroom.

So I went to Wall Street. Ten years. $4.5 billion in deals. 22,000 families housed. Prudential. R4 Capital. BridgeInvest. I learned how the system works — and where it breaks.

Now I'm using that expertise to fix what's broken.

At Shieldstone, we acquire and develop workforce housing in markets where public policy creates opportunity. We pair Wall Street capital markets execution with AI-powered investment systems to deliver institutional returns and measurable community impact.

Through GS Residential — our joint venture with Gateway Merchant Banking — we're targeting 2,000+ units over 36 months. At USDV Capital, where I serve as President & CIO, we originate and manage real estate credit across a $1.3B portfolio.

I also host Built Different — a weekly newsletter and podcast breaking down the signal in housing, AI, policy, and impact investing. No buzzwords. No fluff. Just what the people building tomorrow's housing actually need to know.

Strong returns and strong communities are not in conflict. If you're choosing, you're doing it wrong.

  • "Building Tomorrow's Housing Today." — Playfair Display Bold, large, warm white (#f7f4ef)
  • Descriptors (Workforce Housing . Real Estate Credit . FinTech . Media) — Josefin Sans Regular, soft teal (#58ABA8)
  • Accent line — Coral (#F3B8A7), thin horizontal rule between tagline and descriptors

Atmospheric Effects

  • Blur blobs: Light Blue (#95C9E6) at ~25% opacity and Coral (#F3B8A7) at ~20% opacity
  • Creates the same premium, atmospheric depth as the Shieldstone landing page hero

Logo Arrangement

  • Shieldstone — Largest, top of cluster (primary brand)
  • GS Residential, USDV Capital, Built Different — Secondary size
  • All logos in white/light versions against the dark background
  • Arranged in a clean 2x2 grid or vertical stack on the right 40%

Avoid

  • Stock photos, cityscapes, generic skylines
  • Pure white background
  • Cluttered text — let the typography and logos breathe
  • Colors from GS Residential site (navy #1B2A4A) — use Shieldstone palette only

Mobile Crop Note

LinkedIn crops cover photos to center on mobile. Keep tagline text centered or slightly left-of-center. Leave ~50px safe margin on all sides. Logos should not bleed to the right edge.
